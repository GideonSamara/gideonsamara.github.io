About Me

I was born in Toronto, Ontario and moved to Los Angeles, California with my family when I turned 9.
I am currently 23 years-old and have developed an addiction to traveling and exploring new cultures.
My boyfriend Chris is my absolute best friend. We do everything together, from homework to travel.

My ethnic background is Middle Eastern, specifically Lebanese. I speak arabic, but can't read or write it.
I have a degree in Biology from UCLA and am currently studying for a career change, hopefully into web development.
My original plan was to become a doctor and potentially save lives; however, after my internship, I changed my mind.

Currently, I run two business. A luxury hair care line, and a Life Insurance Agency.
Both of these jobs are very rewarding in different ways. I love helping people, and I get
to do so with beauty and finances. These ventures are both very recent for me, but I love
both these opportunities endlessly.
